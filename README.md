# Case-competition----Four-Consulting-
A 180-minute business case competition involving data analysis, presentation preparation, and Q&amp;A.
## Overview
This project presents my work from an OTAA business case competition completed within 180 minutes. The competition involved analyzing a business case, preparing presentation slides, and answering questions during the Q&A session.

## My Contribution
I was mainly responsible for the data analysis part of the case. Using Excel, I helped organize and interpret the information provided in the case and used the results to support our team’s recommendations. I also assisted in preparing the presentation within the time limit.

- Presentation Support
- Time Management
